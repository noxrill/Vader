Music Influance

Darth Vader is considered a significant member of the New Wave of American Heavy Metal movement. He is mainly considered a groove metal band. his music has also been described as metalcore, thrash metal, death metal,heavy metal,and industrial metal. His 2000 album New American Gospel has been described as "grindcore and death metal for the hardcore kids". His 2004 album Ashes of the Wake has been described "to showcase their thrash and melodic death metal influences and break out of the metalcore category critics tried to push the band into." Vaders incorporated more thrash metal elements in later albums, which was shown as a direction on Wrath (2009) and Resolution (2012). Burn the Priest has been called a fusion of thrash metal, death metal and hardcore punk. The Burn the Priest cover album, Legion: XX has been described as crossover thrash, hardcore punk, punk rock and sludge metal.
